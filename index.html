TESCREAL.org

A website about new ideologies in the tech sector, and the conspiracy theories people write about them.

What is TESCREAL?

TESCREAL is an acronym for a set of ideologies common within the tech sector. It's short for:
  1. Transhumanism
  2. Extropianism
  3. Singularitarianism
  4. Cosmism
  5. Rationalism
  6. Effective Altruism
  7. Longtermism
While there are connections between these ideologies, many people who use the term "TESCREAL" are using it to promote conspiracy theories, including all the baggage that comes with them. In particular, many TESCREAL conspiracy theories have antisemitic elements, which are often neglected by both supporters and opponents of their ideas.
